My main research interest are high latitude climate and ocean dynamics. In terms of the high latitude climate, I am continuing some of the work started on my PhD and trying to understand why there is a such a large model spread on future projections of high latitude climate. In terms of ocean dynamics I work with the MicroInverse method, estimating the ocean lateral sub-gridscale diffusivity, and parameterizing the sub-gridscale diffusivity with a neural-network that uses MicroInverse estimates as a training data.
