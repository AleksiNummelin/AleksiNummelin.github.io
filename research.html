My main research interests at them moment are: effects of the freshwater in the Arctic stratification and sea ice, Arctic cold halocline, shelf water formation in the Arctic, and tracer modelling of Arctic river runoff.

Recently I've also developped some interest on SST reemergence and the possible changes in the future reemergence patterns. I'm also looking into changes in the ocean heat flux entering the Arctic Ocean, check out some CMIP5 simulations here.
